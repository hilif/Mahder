About Me

Mahder Never Dreamed To Be A Programmer!

I wanted to be an “Actress”, like the characters I saw every day on the television screen and theatre halls of Ethiopia. I coveted their fancy potential, shiny suits and clever nicknames, I even started calling Myself “Actress MakiEla ”. I had even enrolled at Dr. Tesfaye's Art school and Birana film production of Fine Arts and there I discovered some basic acting techniques. Unfortunately, my family wanted me to join information technology department and become good programmer. Then I joined department of Information System at Addis Ababa University and, become familiar with programming things and technology, Consequently I loved it and wanted to learn more about it. I am much interested in developing frontend website. I have a serious passion for UI effects, animations and creating intuitive, dynamic user experiences. I wish I will be Well-organised person, problem solver, independent employee with high attention to detail. And, After my graduation I am going to join theatrical art school and become a great actress too!
